Final Section

Summary of data communication and satellite communication

Data Communication

Data communication is the movement of information between two or more devices through a medium. It uses messages, senders, receivers, protocols, and channels to make communication accurate, reliable, secure, and fast.

Satellite Communication

Satellite communication uses orbiting satellites to relay signals between earth stations. It supports broadcasting, GPS, remote internet access, emergency response, aviation, maritime systems, and global communication where cables cannot easily reach.

Importance

These technologies power modern learning, business, mobile networks, banking, streaming, healthcare, security systems, and everyday digital life. They make the world more connected and responsive.
